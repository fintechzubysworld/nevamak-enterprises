📊 Permission Definitions

The following permissions are used across the system:

  • viewDashboard – Access to main dashboard
  • createDispatch – Create new shipments/dispatches
  • viewShipments – View all shipments (filtered by branch)
  • receiveAbuja – Receive goods in Abuja
  • receivePH – Receive goods in Port Harcourt
  • viewReports – Access reports & analytics
  • manageUsers – Create/edit/disable users
  • manageSuppliers – Add/edit suppliers
  • manageInventory – Edit inventory
  • viewInventory – View inventory
  • rerouteInvoice – Reroute invoices to vendors
  • requestInvoice – Request invoices from branches
  • processReturns – Process returns
  • viewInvoiceRepo – View invoice repository
  • managePermissions – Edit permissions (this page)
  • chat – Access chat
